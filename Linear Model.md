
- $X^T = (X_1, X_2, \dots, X_p)$ - vector of inputs
- $Y$ - output
    $$
    \hat{Y} = \hat{\beta}_0 + \sum_{j=1}^p X_j\hat{\beta}_j \hspace{10em}
    $$
- $\hat{\beta}_0$ - intercept / [[Bias|bias]]

### Vector form

- same thing as an [[Inner Product|inner product]]:
    $$
    \hat{Y} = X^T \hat{\beta}
    $$
